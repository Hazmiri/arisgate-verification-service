# Reflection Index – ArisGate

## Purpose
This folder contains reflective evidence for the ArisGate project. The reflections use Rolfe et al.’s reflective model: **What? So What? Now What?** This structure helps me explain what happened, why it mattered, and what I would do next.

The reflections are written in a personal and developmental style because this project involved technical learning, debugging, uncertainty, and professional growth. They are intended to show how my understanding changed during the project, not only what I produced at the end.

## Reflection model used
Rolfe et al.’s reflective model asks three questions:

- **What?** – What happened?
- **So What?** – Why did it matter?
- **Now What?** – What will I do next?
